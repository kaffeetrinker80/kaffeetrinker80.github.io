Personal Tool Hub

Simple, structured and still evolving.

This is the central landing page for my browser-based tools and small projects. The focus is on practical workflows, clean structure and lightweight tools that are easy to open and use on desktop and mobile.

🔒 Strict Privacy & Data Security Although these tools are hosted on GitHub, your data is never uploaded there. Whenever you open a file or import data, it is read strictly locally inside your own browser on your own device. The data never leaves your device and is not sent to me, to GitHub, or to any external server.
📱 Tablet & Mobile Experience If you are not working on a PC or laptop, you can still get the best experience on a tablet. Simply use your browser's zoom function (e.g., zoom out to 75% or 80%) to fit the interfaces perfectly onto your screen.
